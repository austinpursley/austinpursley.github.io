DEFINITIONS OF FRENCH TOAST:
webster dict: "bread dipped in a mixture of egg and milk and sautéed"
dictionary.com: "bread dipped in a batter of egg and milk and sautéed until brown, usually served with syrup or sprinkled with sugar and cinnamon."
cambrdige: "bread that has been covered in egg and fried"
wikipedia: "French toast is a dish made of sliced bread soaked in beaten eggs and typically milk, then pan fried."
