Lichtquanta: Arduino Powered Light Sculpture



I thought it would be fun to do something with LEDs and Arduino. The concept I had in mind for awhile was some sort of light sculpture that could decorate a room. This idea was floating in limbo until I bought a shelf from IKEA that inspired me to go ahead and make something real. You can see the original sketch I did below. The LEDs would be on top of tubes sticking out of the shelf and light up. I also wanted there to be a button that you could press to change between the different animations. The name "Lichtquanta" (German for "quanta of light") comes from Albert Einstein, who used it to describe photons.
