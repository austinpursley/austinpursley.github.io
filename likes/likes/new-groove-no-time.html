“[On Emperor’s New Groove,] we were writing in real time. In a normal four-year process, you’ve got meetings, you’ve got development people going, “What if the girl was a boy? What if the bird was a flower?” And then you have to run all those ideas. But we didn’t have any time. They had to leave us alone. It was the greatest thing in the world.”

An oral history of The Emperor’s New Groove
