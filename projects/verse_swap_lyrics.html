Fly me to the moon

Lets split it into phonemes: 

['F L AY1', 'M IY1', 'T UW1', 'DH AH0', 'M UW1 N']

Find some new word boundaries:

['F L AY1', 'M IY1 T UW1', 'DH AH0', 'M UW1 N']

Replace some of the phonemes...

['F AH1 N D', 'R IY2 V Y UW1', 'T UW1', 'M EY1 N']

... and get the corresponding words.
